**What is Neighbour Cares about?**

Neighbour Cares hopes to build a community of volunteers to check in on seniors (**with a focus on isolated seniors living in rental flats**) regularly to monitor their well-being and take note of signs of distress. Akin to how a good neighbour will care for the people around, volunteers are to befriend the seniors and encourage them to be more engaged with their community.Â
