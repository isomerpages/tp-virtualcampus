<h3><strong>Sports Appreciation Dinner 2026</strong></h3>
<p>What a phenomenal season!</p>
<p>Some 500 student-athletes, alumni, coaches and staff advisors came together
to close off a fulfilling AY2025/26 sporting season in style ‚Äì and to celebrate

the <strong>Student Development &amp; Alumni Affairs Department</strong> in
TP.</p>
<p>AY2025/26 also marked an exciting sporting calendar for TP, where we were
