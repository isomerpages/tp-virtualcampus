<p>All students of the School of Humanities &amp; Social Sciences (HSS) are
part of the HSS Studies Club, a vibrant community at the heart of the student
experience. Guided by the HSS Student Development HEARTS framework, the
Club creates opportunities for students to grow with empathy, think with
purpose, and make a meaningful difference in the lives of others:</p>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Human-Centered Leaders</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Empowered Individuals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Active Well-being</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflective Thinkers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Togetherness</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Service to Community</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Beyond the classroom, students celebrate their shared heritage, stretch
their potential, build meaningful friendships across diverse communities,
give back through service, and discover their strengths as emerging leaders.
It is more than a series of activities; it is a journey of growth, connection,
and impact.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Some of the activities and programmes organised by the Club include the
following:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Freshmen Orientation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Alumni Engagement and Career Talks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mental Wellness Initiatives</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Local Community Projects</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Leadership Training Workshops and Camps</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
