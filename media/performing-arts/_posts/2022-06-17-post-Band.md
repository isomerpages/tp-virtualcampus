<p>Temasek Polytechnic Band (TP Band) is a dynamic performing ensemble of
passionate student musicians, contributing actively to TP’s arts scene
since 1990. TP Band performs a wide repertoire spanning classical wind
literature, contemporary works, popular music and cinematic soundtracks,
and is known for delivering polished, high-energy concerts for both campus
and community audiences.</p>
<p></p>
<p>With a rich blend of woodwind, brass, percussion and strings, TP Band
produces a full, vibrant sound that keeps performances exciting and audience-ready.
Under the baton of Mr Tan Beng Wee (Head Conductor) and Mr Ignatius Wang
(Associate Conductor and alumnus, Music Director – SAF Central Band), members
receive structured training through full rehearsals and sectionals. The
band is a Gold-award winning ensemble at regional band festivals and competitions.</p>
