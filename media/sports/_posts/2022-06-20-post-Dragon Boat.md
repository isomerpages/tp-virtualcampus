<p>Originating from China, dragon boat racing is a human-powered boat sport.
For competitive events, dragon boats are typically rigged with decorative
and colorful Chinese dragon heads and tails. In races, a standard boat
consists of 20 paddlers, while a small boat consists of 10 paddlers, excluding
the steersperson and drummer.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Dragon boat races are a highlight of the Freshmen Orientation (Week Zero)
activities. The TP Dragon Boat Teams (Men &amp; Women) compete annually
in the Polytechnic-ITE (POL-ITE) Dragon Boat Race as well as external competitions.
