<p>Temasek Polytechnic Astronomy aims to nurture our students' love for astronomy.
The club does so by providing accessible opportunities for stargazing,
learning about astronomy, and building connections between like-minded
peers about the subject.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Raising awareness and exposing students to the beauty of astronomy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Creating a relaxed and enriching environment for students interested in
astronomy through bi-weekly stargazing sessions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Organising and participating in astronomy-related events</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Partnering with external agencies and other schools to experience a wider
range of astronomy-related activities.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Planned activities:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Bi-weekly stargazing sessions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>TP-wide events (e.g. CCA Fair)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sub-Committee Bonding Day</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
